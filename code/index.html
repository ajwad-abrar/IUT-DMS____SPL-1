HALLS OF RESIDENCE

IUT provides residential facilities that is safe and comfortable and in great locations inside the University campus.

South Hall

South hall is the oldest hall in IUT. The view from south hall of residence is fabulous. It is a five storey building where every floor has 30 rooms. Four students can live in a room. So, a maximum of 600 students can be accommodated in this hall.

North Hall

North hall is the 2nd oldest hall in IUT. It is also a five storey building where every floor has 30 rooms. Four students can live in a room. The cafeteria for residential students is located at the ground floor of North hall of residence.

Female Hall

Residential facility for female students has been extended. Most of the female students are expected to avail residential facility but not all. Therefore allotmentof seats in the female halls of residence will be based on the policy adopted by IUT.

Facilities

Facilities a student can get after getting into the dorm in IUT

Electricity

Residential students under IUT halls will not experience any load shedding at all. IUT provides 24 * 7 electricity. So, students don't need to think about load-shedding. They can always focus on their study.

Cafeteria

IUT has two self-service Cafeterias where the students take their breakfast and normal meals. All residential students are provided with continental breakfast, lunch, evening tea and dinner.

Security

The security provided in the dormitories by IUT is top-notch. That is why students living in IUT dorms are safe & always under the supervision of IUT. Security is always a big deal for dormitories.

IUT DMS is basically a web based application for maintaining the dormitory system digitally. Every year many students get into IUT & start staying in the hall, get notified about hall notices, inform the authority about any problem and do other activities. It is a platform which is built to put these activities in an automated way.
